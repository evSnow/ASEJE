Tips and Tricks

VS Code has a Variety of features that arent immediately apparent. This page will detail some of the most useful features.

Split Tabs

By dragging the tabs at the top of your screen it will split the screen between the 2 tabs letting you type while also looking at a reference.

Not only can you split the screen horizontally but also vertically! Experiment to find the layout thats best for you.

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • ctrl+k ctrl+f Will automatically format selected text and help to improve readability.
  • ctrl+f Will search for a given string of characters in the file.
  • ctrl+h Will let you replace any instance of a given string of characters with another. Particularly helpful if you need to rename a variable or function.
  • ctrl+k ctrl+s Will display all shortcuts and even allow you to change the keybinds.

Themes

Themes can help reduce eyestrain and made different parts of code stand out so you can tell at a glance whats a variable, comment, or function.
Type '>Preferences: Color Theme' in the search bar or press ctrl+k ctrl+t to view all your themes and choose your favorite.

You can also download new themes from the extention marketplace!
