# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or skill level.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful and supportive.
- Giving constructive feedback.
- Helping others understand the project.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment or discrimination.
- Personal attacks.
- Disruptive comments.

## Our Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying community standards and addressing inappropriate behavior.

## Enforcement
Instances of abuse or unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project team.  
All complaints will be reviewed and handled appropriately.

---

This Code of Conduct helps keep the project respectful and welcoming.
